About

"Coffee is not just a commodity — it is Ethiopia's greatest gift to the world."

With over 30 years dedicated to Ethiopian coffee excellence, Abebe Gebremichael has built a career at the intersection of quality, science and global trade. As a Certified Q-Grader recognised by the Coffee Quality Institute USA, he evaluates every cup with scientific precision — scoring fragrance, aroma, flavour, acidity, body and balance across Ethiopia's finest origins.

From coordinating Harar's regional inspection center for a decade to serving as Acting Director of Marketing at ECTA, Abebe has shaped the systems that bring Ethiopian coffee to tables worldwide. He has represented his country at exhibitions in the USA, Italy, France and Morocco — always with one mission: to show the world what Ethiopian coffee truly is.
